First Win, Pretty U

May 4 2016

Seventeen after a whole year after debut, finally won their first award with their title song Pretty U, Love Letter.

Album of the Year at the 2019 Asia Artist Awards (AAA)

Decemeber 2019

Seventeen, after missout out on the Top Proze at award ceremonies for four years, they finally won its first Daesang at the Asia Artist Awards in 2019. They received their Album of the Year award for their third Full Album, An Ode.

Kings of Synchronization

June 2021

Seventeen was recognized by many of their synchronization when performing their choreography. With a large group of 13 members, that was something hard to achieve. In 2021, Seventeen practice videos were examined through many eyes and computers and it resulted in a 98% synchronization average.

BooSeokSoon (BSS)

February 2023

BooSeokSoon, the only sub-unit of Seventeen, has reached the charts and broken many records from their firsy single album Second Wind on February 6, 2023. "Fighting" was the title song and has featured the rapper, Lee Youngji. The group is made of three of the most animated and energetic members, Boo Seungkwan (Seungkwan), Lee Seokmin (DK), and Kwon Sooyoung (Hoshi). The song, "Fighting", shows the members empathizing with the struggles and stresses of daily life but pledge to encourage and cheer for the listener. "Fighting" in Korean is a phrase to say to someone when you are cheering for them to continue and never give up.

Billboard

May 2023

Seventeen charted at No.1, dated May 13 2023, in Billboards Artist 100 in the Unite States, thanks to the group's new mini album, FML. The set debuts at No.1 on World Albums, and No.2 on the Billboard 200.

Breaking Records

April/September 2023

On April 2023, Seventeen's album distributor YG PLUS, announced that the group's 10th mini album "FML", had achieved an impressive total of 4 million stock pre-orders breaking the record of Kpop pre-orders and on September 2023, another announcement was made of Seventeen's 11th mini album "Seventeen Heaven", reaching 5.2 million stock pre-orders.
